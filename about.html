Our mission is to promote girls making music, art, change, kinship and space for themselves.

Confidence, creativity and noise!

We hosted the first Glasgow summer school in 2015 based on the camps that have been running in America since 2001 - see http://www.girlsrockcamp.org

We need volunteers to buddy, lead workshops, donate instruments and spread the word. We are LGBTQ friendly.

Plans for this summer are on the way!
